Kala Ghoda

Arts Festival 2023

The festival sections are visual arts, dance, music, theatre, cinema, literature including children's literature as a sub section, workshops, heritage walks, urban design and architecture (2014), food, a dedicated section for children, and a vibrant street section including stalls selling eco friendly, hand made arts and crafts wares. It is an iconic art and craft gathering of Asia, organized by the Kala Ghoda Association, a non-profit organization. The festival is a gathering that reflects plenty of events under the theme of history, art, and craft.
